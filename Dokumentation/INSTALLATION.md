# Installation Guide for PWM Fan Controller

## Requirements
- PWM fan controller
- Compatible fans
- Power supply
- Wires and connectors

## Installation Steps
1. **Gather all components:** Ensure you have all necessary components ready.
2. **Connect the fans to the PWM controller:**
   - Follow the wiring diagram provided with your PWM fan controller.
   - Ensure that the fans are connected to the designated PWM outputs.
3. **Connect the power supply:**
   - Connect the power supply to the PWM controller.
   - Ensure that the supply voltage matches the requirements of your fans and controller.
4. **Mount the PWM controller:**
   - Find a suitable location to mount the PWM controller.
   - Secure it in place to prevent movement.
5. **Test the setup:**
   - Power on the system and check if the fans are operating as expected.
   - Adjust fan speeds using the controller settings.

## Troubleshooting
- If the fans do not spin, check all connections.
- Ensure the power supply is functioning properly.
- Refer to the controller manual for specific troubleshooting tips.
